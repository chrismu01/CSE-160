Welcome to the corn maze! Try to reach the other side to collect your corn on the cob!
Along the way you may run into some animals, that means you are heading in the right direction.

How to use this program-
W - move the camera forward;
A - move the camera to the left;
S - move the camera backwards;
D - move the camera to the right;
Q - rotate camera to the left
E - rotate camera to the right
To rotate- click and drag on the canvas and release when rotated to desired place.


NOTES TO GRADERS
I have two textures, corn for the wall and dirt for the ground.
The sky is made of a blue cube
The game of this world is to navigate through the cornmaze to collect your corn on the cob at the end.
Animals are placed along the way as indicators you are going in the right way to the goal
